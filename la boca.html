LA
BOCA

La Boca have the rare, impressive ability to create designs and artwork resembling some of the best album sleeves lurking in your dad’s record collection. Founded in 2002, the studio is based in west London and has gathered an impressive collection of awards, including Cannes Lions, European Design Awards and the AOI Illustration Awards. Their exquisitely executed psychedelic style is the result of each studio member’s unique influences, with the team coming from London, Bordeaux, Devon and Switzerland. Working collaboratively across their studios in London and Amsterdam, La Boca create retina-teasing, tactile and occasionally slightly wonky visual worlds.
