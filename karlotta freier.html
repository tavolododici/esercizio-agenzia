KARLOTTA
FREIER

Karlotta Freier is an accomplished Brooklyn-based artist with a strong background in illustration. In 2021 she graduated from the MFA Illustration as Visual Essay program at the School of Visual Arts, further developing her distinctive visual storytelling style. Alongside her artistic practice, Karlotta is passionate about education and has taught illustration at the China Academy of Art while also speaking as a guest lecturer at universities across the United States. Her work has been exhibited internationally through solo exhibitions, group shows and festivals, earning recognition from the Art Directors Club with both a Cube award in 2018 and a Pencil for Best Illustration Portfolio in 2021.
